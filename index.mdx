## Welcome to Tab Closer

Tab Closer is a lightweight Chrome extension that helps you maintain a clean and organized browser by automatically managing your open tabs. Set a maximum tab limit and let Tab Closer handle the rest.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Automatic Tab Management" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Tab Closer automatically monitors your open tabs and closes the oldest ones when you exceed your configured limit. No manual intervention required.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Configurable Limits" icon="gauge-high">
    Set your preferred maximum tab count (minimum 2 tabs) to match your browsing style and system resources.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Toggle Control" icon="toggle-on">
    Enable or disable automatic tab closing with a simple toggle switch. Perfect for when you need temporary unlimited tabs.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Lightweight & Fast" icon="bolt">
    Built with vanilla JavaScript, Tab Closer has minimal impact on browser performance while providing powerful tab management.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Simple Interface" icon="window">
    Clean popup interface makes configuration quick and intuitive. No complex settings to navigate.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
